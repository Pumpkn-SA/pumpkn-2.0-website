FAQ

Business funding FAQ

Answers for South African SMEs

To qualify, your business must be CIPC-registered, have a business bank account, and have traded for at least 1 year (food businesses) or 3 years (agri businesses). You'll also need a minimum annual turnover of R1m.

We'll need a copy of the business owner's ID, your CIPC registration document, and 6 months of recent business bank statements. You'll also complete our quick onboarding form to get started.

Our loans are designed to help established agri and food businesses manage cash flow and support growth. They can be used for working capital, stock purchases, or day-to-day expenses.

Unfortunately not at this stage - we don't fund startups, offer grants, or finance long-term purchases like land or motor vehicles. Our loans are strictly commercial and carry standard fees.

Once we've received your documents and completed our review, approved businesses can typically access funding within a few business days.
