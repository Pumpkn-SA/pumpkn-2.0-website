Our Cause

Across Africa, a massive $500 billion funding gap is holding back small and medium businesses. They have the potential, but lack access to finance, making growth and tackling climate change a real struggle.
‍
This hits the agriculture and food sector especially hard, these businesses are vital for jobs and our economy, yet traditional lenders often see them as too risky.

That's where Pumpkn steps in. We're bridging that gap, providing accessible finance to the businesses that need it most. We see their potential, believe in their dreams, and we're here to help them thrive.

Our Product Suite

At Pumpkn, we're building the tech to revolutionise how SME's access capital. Our AI-powered funding ladder develops rich credit risk data, unlocking repeated cycles of capital for SMEs to grow.

We need talented individuals like you to help us make it happen. You'll have the opportunity to work with innovative technology, and make a real difference in the lives of entrepreneurs across the continent.
